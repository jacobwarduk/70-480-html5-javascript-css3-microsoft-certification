1. You want to add a comment to your style sheet that says “temporary.” Which is the proper line to add to the CSS file?
C. /* temporary */

2. You want to maintain separation between structure and presentation. How do you create your style rules?
C. Use external style sheets.

3. You want your printer.css style sheet to target printers. How would you set up the style sheet link?
B. <link rel=’stylesheet’ type=’text/css’ href=’Content/printer.css’ media=’print’ />
